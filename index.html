Night Shift / Custom Day Start

Set when your day starts. Entries logged before this time belong to the previous day's window.

Example: If you set Day Start to 18:00 (6 PM), then anything logged at 2AM on June 9th will be counted as June 8th's data — your full 24h window from 6PM Jun 8 to 6PM Jun 9.
